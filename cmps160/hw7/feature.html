Points Attempted

  • Assignment 7: Be able to pan the camera. That is, move the camera along the XY-plane without changing the direction of the camera.
  • Assignment 7: Be able to move the camera forward and backward without changing the camera direction.
  • Assignment 7: Be able to zoom in and out.
  • Assignment 7: Lighting should match camera changes.
  • Assignment 7: Be able to turn the camera left and right (about the y-axis).
  • Assignment 7: Be able to rotate the camera about the z-axis (spin).
  • Assignment 7: Be able to make the camera shake with an animation.
  • Assignment 7: Be able to stretch the scene with the camera.
